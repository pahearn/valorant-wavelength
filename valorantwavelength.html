How to play

The computer has picked a random number X between 1 and 10.

Using this number X, the computer has selected four hints that are an X/10 in the category.
Your goal is to guess what the number is using the hints provided.
You will be given a Map, Weapon, Ultimate Ability, and an Agent.
For each number, there are more than one possible hints in each category.
For example, a 9 could give you a Phantom, Vandal, Odin, or Operator as the weapon.
Each hint can also correspond to more than one number.
For example, the Vandal can be an 8, 9 or 10.
This was done to increase replayability and simulate the variety in answers that you would get from playing with another person
